List of Cryptid Locations

The Mothman

This entity has been located in and around the area of Point Pleasant, West Virginia, USA. It was most often seen around the old West Virginia Ordnace Works, known to locals as the "TNT Area".

The Nameless thing of Berkely Square

This entity inhabits the second floor of number 50, berkely square, London, UK. The Building is now a Bookshop, though staff are told not to climb to the 2nd floor.

The Entity of Tsarichina

This entity is located beneath the ground near the village of Tsarichina, Sofia, Bulgaria. The site was concreted over by the bulgarian military after the associated incident.
