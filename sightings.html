Sightings of the Strange

List of Sightings

The Mothman

November 12th, 1966, near Clendenin, West Virginia by a group of five men who repoted a humanoid creature in the trees that then flew away.

November 15th, 1966, TNT Area, north of Point Pleasant, Westvirginia by two couples when the creature persued them down Highway 62 in their car at speeds of over 100mph.

Over 100 adiitional sighting were reported upto December 15th, 1957 when the Silver Bredge Collapsed.

The Nameless Thing of Berkely Square

The first incident occured in 1840, when robert warboys slept on the second floor for a dare. An hour later he was found in the corner of his bedroom, scared out of his wits and with a discharged pistol still raised in self-defence.

In December 1887, Two sailors from HMS Penelope slept in the building, only to be attacked in the night by a creature. One of the sailors went to ge help, When he returned with a police officer they found his friend dead outside the front of the building.

The Entity of Tsarichina

The entity was encountered in 1992, in an unground chamber by the bulgarian military during an excavtion project in the area.

After the encounter the Bulgarian Military abandoned the project, sealed the tunnel, and covered the area with concrete.
